Local PDF Extractor

Extract text from typed, scanned & handwritten PDFs

Settings

Max accuracy is slower but renders at higher DPI and uses angle correction.

Advanced preprocessing

Deskew, contrast-boost & denoise before OCR. Best left on.

Force black-and-white. Helps some clean/high-contrast scans; usually leave off (grayscale is more accurate on most documents).

Re-read every page as an image, even pages that already have selectable text. Use only when the embedded text is wrong/garbled, or text is trapped inside images. Slower; off by default.

Strip repeating page headers, footers & page numbers from multi-page documents (3+ pages). On by default; turn off to keep every line exactly as on the page.

Handwriting & online OCR

Read handwritten pages with a local model (English, ~73% on real cursive). Much slower; first use downloads a model once. For far better handwriting, use Online Gemini below instead — you don't need both.

Local handwriting isn't installed in this build — use Online vision OCR (Gemini) below for handwriting.

Far higher accuracy on handwriting & hard scans, via Google Gemini. Sends your page images to Google — off by default. Needs a free API key.

This demo includes a Gemini key — just turn this on and leave the key field blank. Or paste your own key to use that instead.

Your key is stored only in this browser and sent with each upload — never saved on the server. Pages with a real text layer are still read locally (not sent).

Good to know
  • Typed / born-digital pages are extracted instantly and exactly — no OCR.
  • OCR accuracy depends on scan quality; handwriting is the hardest.
  • For the best handwriting, use Online Gemini; everything else runs on your machine.
  • The first local-OCR run downloads model weights once, then caches them.
